YouCube NY Meetup

@YouCubeMeetup

With the recent post-poning of Mineorama a group of community members, YouTubers, and server hosts have came together with the intention of making the most of the situation.

YouCube Meetup is the result of that drive to repair what was made wrong. On July 12th we'll be hosting a fan meetup with those who originally intended to go to Mineorama. No charge. No catch. Open to everyone. We're currently looking for a facility to facilitate the event and we'll be making announcements on our Twitter as we know more.
